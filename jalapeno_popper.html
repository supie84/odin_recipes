Jalapeno Poppers

Description

Jalapeno poppers are jalape√±o peppers that have been hollowed out, stuffed with a mixture of cheese, spices, and sometimes ground meat, and breaded and deep fried. They can also be wrapped in various meats and paired with various sauces. Jalapeno poppers make a great appitizer and party finger food.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package of cream cheese, softened
  • 1 (8 ounce) package of shredded sharp cheddar cheesee
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 15 fresh jalapeno peppers, halved length wise and seeded
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 tablespoon milk
  • 1 1/2 cups curshed corn flake cereal

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly grease a medium baking sheet.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix together cream cheese, sharp cheddar chees, and mayonnaise. Stuff jalapeno halves with this mixture
  3. Whisk together eggs and milk in a small bowl. Place crushed corn flake cereal in separate small bowl.
  4. Dip each stuffed jalapeno into egg and milk mixture, then roll in corn cereal to coat.
  5. Arrange jalapenos in single layer on the prepared baking sheet. Bake in the preheated oven 30 minutes, or until filling is bubbly and lightly browned.
