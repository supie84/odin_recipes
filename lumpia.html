Lumpia

Description

Lumpia are various types of spring rolls commonly found in the Philippines and Indonesia. Lumpia are made of thin paper-like or crepe-like pastry skin called "lumpia wrapper" enveloping savory or sweet fillings. An egg roll wrapper is often used for lumpia.

Ingredients

  • 3 packages of egg roll wrappers
  • 5 lbs ground beef
  • 1 16 ounce bag of frozen peas
  • 1 16 ounce bag of frozen carrots, chopped
  • Seasoning salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Garlic powder to taste
  • Vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Brown hamburger meat in large skillet and drain grease
  2. Combine peas and carrots with meat and cook until warm
  3. Add seasoning to taste
  4. Get a small bowl with cool water
  5. Lay out egg roll wrapper and put 1 tablespoon of meat filling in the center towards one corner, wrap the corner over the meat, and roll tightly. Dip finger into water and wipe over free corner to seal.
  6. Repeat previous step until all lumpia are rolled
  7. Pour oil into deep pot and heat on high heat
  8. Once oil is sizzling hot put lumpia in pot in single layer and fry on each side until golden brown.
  9. Take lumpia out and place on paper towel to cool
