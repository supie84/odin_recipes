Deviled Eggs

Description

Deviled eggs are hard-boiled eggs that have been shelled, cut in half, and filled with a paste made from the egg yolks mixed with other ingredients such as mayonnaise and mustard.They are generally served cold as a side dish, appetizer or a main course during gatherings or parties

ingredients

  • 6 large eggs hard-boiled
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon favorite mustard
  • 1 tablespoon favorite sweet relish
  • 1 teaspoon seasoning salt
  • Paprika to garnish

Directions

  1. Cut hard-boiled eggs in half length wise
  2. Scoop yolk from both side of eggs into a bowl
  3. Once all eggs are cut and yolks are in the bowl mash the yolks with the back of a fork
  4. Add mayonnaise. mustard, sweet relish, and seasoning salt to bowl with smashed yolks
  5. Mix thoroughly by hand or in blender if you desire a more smooth consistancy
  6. Put mixture in a plastic sandwich bag or pipping bag
  7. Put empty egg white halves on a flat serving platter in a single layer
  8. Cut a small hole in the bottom of the plastic bag or pipping bag and squeeze the mixture into the egg white halves until all are filled
  9. Sprinkle paprika on the tops of the egg yolk mixture and serve
